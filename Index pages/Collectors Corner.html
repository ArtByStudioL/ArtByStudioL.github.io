Follow our Blog!
 

 
The Butterfly Handle sets all have a back stamp dating them to 1931.  They  all have the word "Crown" China, which was phased out by 1935 & replaced w/the word "Bone China".  They have become quite rare since the handles are so delicate there aren't a lot left not broken - I am fortunate to have 20 sets.

The Forget-me-not pattern is special to me as it is also the R.A. July Flower of the Month pattern – which is the birth month of my twin girls & we lost one of them when they were 30 yrs old

Karen's Collection

I live in Northern California, just a couple hours from the Oregon order, about 40 miles past Eureka where the giant redwoods are and then you go inland and up in elevation to get to our remote home. I'm up in the mountains on 37 acres on Redwood Creek with my husband, 6 cats, 2 mules and a border collie named Bell. We have 2 grown sons and 1 daughter...and 9 grandkids...8 boys and 1 girl.
I've collected Royal Albert exclusively for about 25 years. My favorite patterns are Night and Day, all the colors of Laurentian Snowdrop, English Beauty with the rainbow colored backgrounds, Yuletide  (with Poinsettia flowers), Blossom Time and many of the very old and hard to find patterns.
I have approximately 300 RA cup and saucer sets and another 75 RA tennis sets.
