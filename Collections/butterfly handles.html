Shirley's Butterfly Handle Collection

The Butterfly Handle sets all have a back stamp dating them to 1931.  They  all have the word "Crown" China, which was phased out by 1935 & replaced w/the word "Bone China".  They have become quite rare since the handles are so delicate there aren't a lot left not broken -
I am fortunate to have 20 sets.
